# Zag Post Values

This document describes the values that Zag Post is archtectured around. These values are the guiding principles that shape our work and culture.

## 1. Privacy

Protecting user data and ensuring privacy is our top priority. We are committed to implementing robust security measures and transparent data practices to safeguard users' information.

## 2. User-Centric Design

We prioritize the needs and experiences of our users in every aspect of our product design and development. We strive to create intuitive, accessible, and enjoyable experiences for all users.

That means, listening to user feedback, conducting user research, and continuously iterating on our designs to meet the evolving needs of our users.
